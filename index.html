About Us

Monterey School is a dedicated educational institution offering quality Christian education, emphasizing 21st-century skills, promoting love, freedom, diversity, and shaping responsible, humanitarian, democratic citizens. Our commitment is to nurture the mind, heart, and spirit of our students, preparing them to excel academically and contribute positively to the world.

Mission

We are commited to provide our students with quality Christian education by developing in them the 21st century skills through globally competitive instructions in an enviroment of freedom, love, peace and responsibility

Vision

A premier institution of learning commited to transform individuals to be ethical Christians and enviromentally responsible, humanitarian, productive and democratic citizens
